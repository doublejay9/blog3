May's got an additional Friday for us this year. So here's an extra idea for your writing habit.

<!--excerpt-->

-------------------------------

A couple land in a tropical paradise, ready to enjoy their honeymoon. But when they arrive at the address for their resort, it’s nowhere to be seen. Locals tell them that a resort never existed on that part of the island. What’s going on? Can they still salvage their honeymoon?
